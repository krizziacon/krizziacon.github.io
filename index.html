About Me

Outside the technical scope, I'm currently a Sustainability Ambassador for the University and a High School Outreach volunteer for UA-WiSE. With these two groups, I am given the opportunity to represent the causes I am passionate about - supporting diversity initiatives and sustainability, all while working with people of variety of backgrounds.

I am an adaptive and collaboarative individual with an interest in roles focused on software development, data analysis, and database management.

Experienced with developing exciting applications and analysis using: Python, SQL, QGIS, HTML/CSS, JavaScript.
